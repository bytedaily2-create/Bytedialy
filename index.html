📰 Top 5 Tech News — March 1, 2026

Hey guys! So I was scrolling through the news this morning with my chai and honestly today is a pretty big day in tech. Let me break it down for you real quick 👇

01
Apple

Apple is dropping new iPhone, iPad & Mac — tomorrow!

Okay so this is big. Apple confirmed that starting March 2 they are releasing a bunch of new products. And the best part? MagSafe is finally coming to ALL iPhones not just the expensive ones. About time honestly. I'm personaly very excited about this one, been waiting for ages!

02
AI

Siri is getting a massive upgrade powered by Google Gemini

Okay I know what you're thinking — Apple and Google working together?? Yeah I was shocked too lol. But apparently the new Siri is going to be completely rebuilt with Google Gemini AI. It will understand your screen, work across apps and actually be useful this time. We'll see how that goes 😅

03
NVIDIA

NVIDIA just made $68.1 Billion in one quarter. Crazy right?

Bro I don't even know what to say. NVIDIA made $68.1 billion in just three months. All because everyone needs their AI chips right now. Companies are literally fighting to buy them. If you invested in NVIDIA two years ago you're probably very happy today 😂

04
OpenAI

OpenAI raised $110 Billion. Yes you read that correctly

So OpenAI just closed a $110 billion funding round backed by Amazon, Nvidia and SoftBank. That is probaly one of the biggest funding rounds in tech history. ChatGPT is not going anywhere anytime soon it seems. The AI race is getting more intense every single day.

05
Samsung

Samsung wants 800 Million AI devices by end of 2026

And finally Samsung announced they want to put AI in 800 million of their devices by end of this year. That's basically putting Google Gemini AI in almost every Samsung phone out there. Honestly the competition between Samsung and Apple right now is getting really interesting to watch!

Anyway that's it for today's news! Let me know in the comments which story surprised you the most. See you tomorrow with more updates 👋
